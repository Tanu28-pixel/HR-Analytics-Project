Attrition by Department Insights
Research & Development department records the highest employee attrition.
Sales department also contributes significantly to workforce turnover.
Human Resources department shows comparatively lower attrition levels.

Attrition by Gender Insights
Attrition occurs across both male and female employees.
Male employees contribute slightly higher attrition counts due to larger workforce representation.

Overtime Analysis Insights
Employees working overtime demonstrate higher attrition trends.
Work-life balance appears to strongly influence employee retention.

Average Salary Insights
Average monthly salary varies across departments.
Certain departments show comparatively higher compensation levels than others.

Overall Business Insights
Employee attrition is influenced by multiple factors including overtime, department workload, and salary distribution.
Workforce analysis highlights the importance of employee satisfaction and retention strategies.
Dashboard visualizations support better HR decision-making and workforce planning.
