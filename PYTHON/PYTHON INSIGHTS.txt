# HR Analytics EDA Insights

## Employee Attrition Insights

* Majority of employees stayed in the company, while a smaller portion experienced attrition.
* Employee attrition is one of the key business concerns identified in the dataset.
* Attrition patterns suggest possible influence of workload, salary, and job satisfaction.

---

# Age Distribution Insights

* Most employees belong to the age group of approximately 30–40 years.
* Very few employees are above 50 years of age.
* Workforce is dominated by mid-career professionals.

---

# Salary Insights

* Monthly income distribution varies significantly among employees.
* Some employees receive comparatively very high salaries while most fall within moderate income ranges.
* Salary imbalance may influence employee retention and satisfaction.

---

# Department Insights

* Research & Development department contains the highest number of employees.
* Sales department also contributes a significant portion of the workforce.
* Human Resources department has the smallest employee count.

---

# Gender Insights

* Male employees are higher in number compared to female employees.
* Gender distribution is not perfectly balanced across the organization.

---

# Overtime Insights

* A considerable number of employees work overtime.
* Employees working overtime show higher attrition patterns compared to employees without overtime.
* Overtime may be a major factor affecting employee retention.

---

# Job Satisfaction Insights

* Employee job satisfaction levels vary across the organization.
* Moderate satisfaction levels appear more common than extremely high satisfaction.
* Lower satisfaction may contribute to employee attrition.

---

# Attrition by Gender Insights

* Attrition occurs across both male and female employees.
* Male employees contribute a slightly higher attrition count due to larger workforce size.

---

# Attrition by Overtime Insights

* Employees working overtime exhibit significantly higher attrition rates.
* Work-life balance appears to impact employee retention strongly.

---

# Overall Business Insights

* Employee attrition is influenced by multiple factors such as overtime, salary, job satisfaction, and department workload.
* Workforce analysis highlights the importance of improving employee satisfaction and reducing overtime burden.
* HR analytics dashboards can support strategic workforce planning and retention decision-making.
