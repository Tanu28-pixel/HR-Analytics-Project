# HR Analytics Project

## Project Overview

The HR Analytics Project is an end-to-end data analytics solution designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, overtime trends, salary distribution, and departmental performance.

The project combines Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to transform raw HR data into actionable business insights that can support workforce planning and employee retention strategies.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition patterns.
* Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
* Evaluate the impact of overtime on retention.
* Examine workforce demographics and departmental distribution.
* Build interactive dashboards for decision-making.
* Generate business insights to improve workforce management.

## Key Insights

### Employee Attrition

* Overall employee attrition rate is approximately 16.12%.
* Attrition remains one of the major workforce challenges.

### Department Analysis

* Research & Development department records the highest attrition.
* Sales department also contributes significantly to turnover.

### Overtime Analysis

* Employees working overtime exhibit higher attrition rates.
* Work-life balance plays a critical role in employee retention.

### Salary Analysis

* Salary distribution varies across departments.
* Compensation differences may impact retention trends.

### Experience Analysis

* Employees with fewer years at company show higher turnover patterns.
* Long-term employees demonstrate stronger retention.
