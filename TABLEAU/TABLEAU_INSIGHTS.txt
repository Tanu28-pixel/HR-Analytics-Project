Attrition by Department Insights
Research & Development department records the highest employee attrition among all departments.
Sales department also contributes significantly to workforce turnover.
Department-level analysis highlights possible workload and retention challenges.

Attrition by Gender Insights
Employee attrition is observed across both male and female employees.
Male employees contribute slightly higher attrition counts due to larger workforce representation.

Overtime Analysis Insights
Employees working overtime exhibit noticeably higher attrition trends.
Overtime patterns indicate potential work-life balance concerns within the organization.

Attrition Trend Insights
Employees with fewer years at company show higher turnover patterns.
Attrition trends gradually decrease as employee experience increases.
Long-term employees demonstrate comparatively better workforce stability.

Overall Business Insights
Workforce attrition is influenced by factors such as overtime, experience level, and department workload.
Tableau visualizations support effective workforce storytelling and trend analysis.
Interactive dashboards help organizations identify retention challenges and improve HR decision-making.
